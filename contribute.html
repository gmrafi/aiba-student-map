About This Project

This project is more than just a map‚Äîit's a living, evolving record of our community. By connecting the journeys of Army IBA Sylhet students from every corner of Bangladesh, we are building a foundation for future activities, collaborations, and opportunities. The map is designed to grow with us, reflecting not only where we come from, but also where we are going. It will support new projects, events, and connections for years to come, making our shared story accessible and meaningful for both current and future generations.

The Army IBA Sylhet Student Map is an interactive web app and visualization showing where our students come from‚Äîacross all of Bangladesh, from BBA 1 to every batch.
