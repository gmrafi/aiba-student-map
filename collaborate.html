What is this project?

The Army IBA Sylhet Student Map is an interactive visualization showing where our students come from‚Äîacross all of Bangladesh, from BBA 7th to 13th batches. Our university is in Sylhet, but our community is nationwide.

  • Understand student distribution and diversity
  • Explore travel routes and connections to Sylhet
  • Support research into educational access and student journeys
  • Plan future initiatives for students and the institution
